Polenta

A heart warming filling dish that can be easily made days before use.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken broth
  • Polenta
  • Salt
  • Any meltable cheese
  • Ham

Method:

  1. Add the polenta and chicken broth to a pan
  2. Cook the polenta as the specific packaging requires and to desired consistency
  3. Add the cheese just before the polenta is finished cooking
  4. Stir in the ham
  5. Pour into a brownie pan and flatten with a spatula
  6. Leave overnight in the fridge
  7. Cut polenta into large rectangles and eat as desired cold or warmed up in a microwave

Storage life:

Store in the fridge. Will last for 5 days or until it starts to smell bad, taste bitter, or change colour.
