Polenta

A heart warming filliing dish that can be easily made days before use.

Ingredients:

For the dressing;

  • Miso
  • Dashi stock powder
  • Soy sauce

For the salad;

  • Courgette
  • Carrots
  • Eggs
  • Roasted pinenuts
  • Spring onions

Method:

  1. Spiralise the carrots and courgettes
  2. Add the eggs to boiling water and allow to cook for 8 minutes
  3. Slice the egg and add it in a bowl with the spiralised veg
  4. Dice the spring onion
  5. Add any other protein/veg as desired
  6. Add all of the sauce ingredients together with boiling water and mix well
  7. Add the sauce to the salad
  8. Top the salad with the pinenuts and spring onion
  9. Serve and enjoy

Storage life:

Store the vegtable and sauce seperate, combining just before serving. Sauce will last a 5 days in the fridge. Salad is best eaten on the day made.
