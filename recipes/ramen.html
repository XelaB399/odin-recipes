Ramen

A very quick filling and cheap meal to make when your busy.

Ingredients:

For the broth;

  • Miso
  • Dashi stock powder
  • Soy sauce

For the ramen elements;

  • Dehydrated glass noodles
  • Bokchoy
  • Spring onions
  • Cooked chicken breast or canned tuna
  • Sesame seeds

Method:

  1. Hydrate the noodles with boiling water
  2. Add the broth ingrediants into a small amount of boiling water and stir until everything is combined
  3. Add the noodles to the broth base and stir
  4. Add the protein of choice to the noodles
  5. Chop the bokchoy into mouth sized slices, add to the noodles
  6. Add boiling water until the ramen is at desired amount and stir gently
  7. Dice the spring onion
  8. Add the spring onion and sesame seeds to the ramen
  9. Enjoy

Storage life:

Can not be made in advance and will not store well in the fridge. Best to pre prepare the protein and veg to be added to broth and noodles on the day of consumption.
